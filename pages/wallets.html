Bitcoin wallets make payments global, easy, fast, cheap, and secure. More importantly, funds stored in true Bitcoin wallets give you full control of your money. Our comprehensive wallet overviews help you find the Bitcoin wallet to suit your needs.

Cold Storage

Wallets built with security in mind. Your private keys are generated offline in a secure environment. Use cold storage wallets for secure, long term storage.

Hot Wallets

Hot wallets give you full control over your private keys, but aren't built for long term secure storage of funds. Private keys are generated on an internet connected device. Use hot wallets if you use Bitcoin for shopping, and are frequently making payments.
