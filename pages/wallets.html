Bitcoin wallets (what is a Bitcoin wallet?) make payments global, easy, fast, and secure. More importantly, funds stored in true Bitcoin wallets give you full control of your money.

Our wallet overviews help you find a Bitcoin wallet to suit your needs. Please understand that there is no best Bitcoin wallet. Each user is unique and has different needs.

How to Get a Bitcoin Wallet

You can can get a Bitcoin wallet in three different ways: 1) buy a Bitcoin hardware wallet 2) create a Bitcoin wallet online or 3) download a Bitcoin wallet app to your phone, tablet, or computer.

Bitcoin Hardware Wallet Overviews

A Bitcoin hardware wallet is a physical device that is built with the sole purpose of securing Bitcoin private keys. Hardware Bitcoin wallets keep private keys away from internet-connected devices and allow users to sign transactions in an offline environment.
