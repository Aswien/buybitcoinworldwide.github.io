Bitcoin wallets (what is a Bitcoin wallet?) make payments global, easy, fast, and secure. More importantly, funds stored in true Bitcoin wallets give you full control of your money. Our wallet overviews help you find a Bitcoin wallet to suit your needs.

Bitcoin Hardware Wallet Overviews

A hardware wallet is a physical device that is built with the sole purpose of securing Bitcoin private keys. Hardware wallets keep private keys away from internet-connected devices and allow users to sign transactions in an offline environment.

Android Bitcoin Wallets

Android offers developers more freedom than iOS. For example, it's only possible to use hardware wallets with Android Bitcoin wallets, because Apple doesn't allow the use of OTG cables. The best Android Bitcoin wallets let users control their private keys.
