There will likely never be an easy, straight forward way to buy bitcoin with PayPal due to the irreversible nature of Bitcoin transactions.

If you can, transfer your PayPal funds to your bank and buy via bank transfer. Or, transfer your PayPal funds to your bank, withdrawal as cash, and buy bitcoin with cash deposit.
